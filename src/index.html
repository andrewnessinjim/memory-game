Directions

  • This game tests your memorization skills! You will be presented with a grid of cards laid face down. You can click on any card to reveal it. Once you have revealed two cards, they will stay revealed if they are the same card. Otherwise, they will be turned face down again. Your goal is to pair up all matching cards.
  • Revealing two cards is considered as 1 move.
  • The timer will start once you click on the first card.
  • Click on to reveal options menu.
  • Click on in the options menu to read these directions again.
  • Use the button in the options menu to reset the game and start over.
  • Click on to hide options menu. This magnet will appear when you reveal the options menu.
